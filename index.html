CryptoFish is a hand-crafted pixel art NFT collection by Findurnemo, celebrating real fish species and marine biodiversity. About CryptoFish

CryptoFish is a collection of 2,166 hand-crafted pixel art NFTs, each representing a real fish species. Every fish is unique, with rarity based on both collection traits and real-world biology. Created by Findurnemo, CryptoFish celebrates marine biodiversity and the beauty of rare species.

  • Every CryptoFish is a real, scientifically classified species, with genus, species, and locality encoded on-chain.
  • Rarity is not just about collection traits—many fish are rare in the wild due to their conservation status, limited range, or unique evolutionary history.
  • Some species are critically endangered or known from only a single locality, making their digital representation as rare as their real-world existence.
  • Traits like color, pattern, and locality are based on real biology, not just random generation.
  • The collection includes over 1,700 species, 340+ genera, and nearly 1,000 localities, making it one of the most scientifically diverse NFT projects.
  • Each fish is hand-drawn in pixel art, referencing real scientific illustrations and field guides.
  • CryptoFish is a tribute to the diversity and fragility of aquatic life, aiming to raise awareness for conservation through art and technology.

For more, follow @findurnemo and explore the full collection on OpenSea.
